How to use these resources

Each tool is a standalone web app — no login, no account, no installation. Open it in any browser on any device. Your reflection notes save privately to your own device and are never sent anywhere.

The leader formation guide is designed to be completed individually first, then discussed as a group of four. The pre-marriage guide is designed for a couple to work through privately, then bring into a session with their counselor or pastor.
